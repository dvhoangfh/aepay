"Acquaintance" Phuong - Toan "finished" Myanmar, sending threats to Vietnam
#Myanmar
#Vietnam
#CongPhuong
#VanToan

00:06 11/09/2019

Without too many difficulties, Japan easily defeated Myanmar 2 - 0 in the first match of the World Cup qualifying round. Notably, the "old acquaintance" of Cong Phuong and Van Toan - striker Minamino got himself 1 goal.


Possessing outstanding strength, the Japanese team did not have too many difficulties to overwhelm Myanmar. In the 16th minute, midfielder Nakajima showed his class with a wonderful cut, the ball went straight into the net to block goalkeeper Kyaw Zin Phyo, opening the score 1 - 0 for the team from the country of cherry blossoms.
10 minutes later, "Blue Samurai" had a goal to double the lead. And the scorer this time is the star striker Minamino, who is wearing the shirt of Austrian champions Red Bull Salzburg. Japan's next game was quite leisurely.
